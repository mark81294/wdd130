History

Founded in 1990, our company has been at the forefront of white water rafting adventures for over three decades. With a commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship, we ensure every trip is not only thrilling but also enriching. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned adventurer, our experienced guides and top-notch equipment guarantee an unforgettable journey through some of the world's most breathtaking rapids.
