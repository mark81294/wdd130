Overview

Introduction

This section provides an introduction to the whitewater rafting site plan.

Purpose

The purpose of this site plan is to outline the key aspects of establishing and promoting a whitewater rafting business, including target audience, branding, and site design.

Audience

The target audience for the whitewater rafting site includes adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and families looking for outdoor activities. Here are three scenarios:

Scenario 1

Adventure enthusiasts looking for a thrilling experience.

Scenario 2

Families seeking a safe and enjoyable outdoor activity.

Scenario 3

Nature lovers interested in exploring rivers and natural landscapes.
